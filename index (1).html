The Spirit of Pongal

Thai Pongal

The main day of celebration, dedicated to the Sun God. Families gather to cook the sacred Pongal dish as the sun rises, symbolizing abundance and gratitude.

Harvest Festival

Pongal marks the end of the winter solstice and celebrates the harvest season. It's a time to thank nature for its bounty and express gratitude for prosperity.

Tamil Heritage

One of the oldest festivals in South India, Pongal reflects the rich cultural heritage of Tamil Nadu. It brings families together in celebration of life and prosperity.
